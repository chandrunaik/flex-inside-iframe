Dummy CRM or Ticketing System sending and receiving data from Flex.

Sample use cases:

  1. Accepting an inbound call will trigger a postMessage with the customer unique identifier, in this case the phone number.
  2. Hanging up a call will trigger a postMessage passing call metadata.
  3. You can simulate click to dial by filling in the text field below with your number and pressing call (E164 format).
